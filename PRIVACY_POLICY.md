# Privacy Policy for Audio Seeker

Effective date: April 28, 2026

Audio Seeker ("the app") is an Android music player focused on local audio playback. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Process

- **Local media metadata**: The app reads local media information (such as song title, artist, album, and file path) from your device to build your music library and provide playback features.
- **Playback preferences**: The app may store local settings such as equalizer state, playback speed, queue state, and sleep timer preferences.
- **Wallet/Web3 data (optional features)**: If optional Web3 features are enabled and used, wallet address and transaction-related data may be processed to support those features.

## 2. What We Do Not Sell

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not use your local media files for advertising profiling.

## 3. Permissions

Audio Seeker requests only the permissions required for core functionality, such as media/library access and playback-related operations.

## 4. Data Storage

- Core playback preferences are stored locally on your device.
- Local media scanning is performed on-device.
- If optional Web3 features are used in future releases, interactions may involve public blockchain data and third-party wallet providers.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party open-source libraries. If optional Web3 features are enabled, external wallet providers or blockchain infrastructure may apply their own privacy policies.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Audio Seeker is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app data and follow platform security best practices. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in this document with a revised effective date.
